Welcome to ARC-Interactive!

ARC-Interactive is a tool for researchers and enthusiasts working with the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC). It runs in the browser and does not need a backend.

The source code is on GitHub.

Join the community on Discord.

What is ARC?

The Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC) is a groundbreaking dataset aimed at advancing artificial intelligence research by focusing on abstract problem solving and reasoning capabilities. It challenges both humans and AI systems with a variety of tasks that require deep understanding and innovative thinking.
